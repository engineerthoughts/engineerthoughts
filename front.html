MEMORY GAME - DESKTOP VERSION

Game rules:

No one likes rules. Here is basic things you need to know.

1. This is a Desktop based Memory Game, Sorry for the inconvenience (Mobile Users).

2. The chess board consist of various pieces at various positions.

You need to remember the positions of each pieces and you need to assemble them correctly.

3. Level: 1- 2 have (15secs) to memorize the pieces and (20secs) to place them.

Level: 3- 5 have (20secs) to memorize the pieces and (30secs) to place them.

4. Level: 6-15 have (60secs) to memorize the pieces and (90secs) to place them.

5. You can click "READY" button if you have memorized the pieces before time

and you will be directed to the empty chess board where you can place the pieces.

You need to drag and drop the pieces into the correct boxes accordingly.

6. For every Pieces correctly placed the box will turn green and once you finish placing

your coins click "ANALYSIS" button to check your efficiency.

7. As soon as you click the "START" button you will be directed to the FIRST LEVEL.
